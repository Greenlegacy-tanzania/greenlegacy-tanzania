Green Legacy Tanzania

Green Legacy Tanzania was established with a strong commitment to addressing environmental challenges facing communities across Tanzania. We empower youth and women to actively participate in green development and environmental protection.

Learn More About Us

Who We Are

Green Legacy Tanzania is a community-driven environmental organization committed to promoting conservation, climate resilience, and sustainable development.

Our Vision

To build a sustainable society that respects, protects, and utilizes natural resources responsibly for the benefit of present and future generations.

Our Mission

To empower communities to actively participate in environmental conservation through education, campaigns, social projects, and national and international collaboration.

Our Core Objectives

Increase public awareness of environmental conservation and climate change.
Promote tree planting to reduce deforestation and safeguard biodiversity.
Empower youth and women through green employment opportunities.
Promote environmental cleanliness, recycling, and sustainable resource use.
Provide environmental education to schools and universities.

Our Strategic Programs

Green Legacy Tanzania implements data-driven and community-led initiatives to combat climate change and promote ecological resilience across the United Republic of Tanzania.
